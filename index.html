Jaimie Sharp

About

Jaimie has learning difficulties and Autism. He began painting in 2009.

He loves mixing colours and naturally thinks in pictures. By learning to paint; Jaimie is able to share his vision with a wider audience.

Exhibitions

Six works painted by Jaimie, with the theme of ‘Seaside Spectrum’, were on display in May 2012 at Brighton & Hove's ‘Artists Open House’ festival as part of the Hanover Art Trail.
